nib::integrator

[−][src]Module nib::integrator::normal

The normal integrator is a diagnostic integrator that simply returns the normal values of the surfaces it hits.

This is useful to ensure that normals and surface intersections are being calculated correctly, and that the geometry of the scene is correct.
